Influential People in Art, Design, Engineering, and Typography

Art

Artists

Jean-Michel Basquiat

Basquiat was an American artist who rose to fame in New York in the 1980's. His artful employment of contradictory themes, from historicism to street art and abstraction to figuration, secured his enduring legacy as an influential artist.

Salvador Dali

Dali, a Spanish Surrealist, is best known for his precise execution of his bizzare, dream-like imagery.

Frida Kahlo

Kahlo, a Mexican painter working in the early 20th century, was known for her striking self-portraits as well as her depictions of the natural world.

Typography

Typographers

Matthew Carter

A 2005 New Yorker profile described Carter, a typeface designer, as the most widely read man in the world. His designed two of the most widely-used web fonts, Verdana and Georgia.

Susan Kare

Kare, an artist and designer, is known for her typeface contributions to early the Apple Macintosh design interface.

Jan Tschichold

Tschichold was a modernist calligrapher, typographer, and book designer working in the decades following World War II. He designed the Sabon font which is still widely used and admired.
